The rose is a woody perennial flowering plant of the genus Rosa, known for its iconic beauty and fragrant scent. Available in a vast array of colors like deep red and soft pink, roses have been used for centuries to symbolize love, passion, and friendship. Beyond their decorative appeal, they are also valued for their essential oils in perfumes and their petals in culinary applications worldwide. Many species are native to Asia, though they are now grown globally in diverse climates and garden styles.

The rose is a woody perennial flowering plant of the genus Rosa, in the family Rosaceae, known for its iconic beauty and fragrant scent. Available in a vast array of colors like deep red, soft pink, and vibrant yellow, roses have been used for centuries to symbolize everything from love and passion to friendship and peace.
